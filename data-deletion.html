Data Deletion Instructions

Effective Date: October 28, 2025

⚠️ Important: You have the right to request deletion of your personal data at any time. We will process your request within 30 days as required by GDPR and CCPA regulations.

What Data Can Be Deleted

You can request deletion of the following data:

  • Email address and subscription information
  • Name and contact details
  • Email engagement history (opens, clicks)
  • Newsletter interaction data
  • Any social media interaction data we've collected
  • Analytics data associated with your account

Data Deletion Methods

Choose the method that works best for you:

Method 1: Automated Email Unsubscribe (Fastest)

Processing Time: Immediate (automatic)

  1. Open any email from The Data Brief
  2. Scroll to the bottom of the email
  3. Click the "Unsubscribe" link
  4. Your email will be immediately removed from our mailing list
  5. All associated data will be permanently deleted within 7 days

This is the fastest method and requires no manual processing.

Method 2: Email Request (For Complete Data Deletion)

Processing Time: Within 30 days

  1. Send an email to: victor@videbimusai.com
  2. Subject line: "Data Deletion Request - The Data Brief"
  3. Include in your email:
    • Your email address used for subscription
    • Your name (if provided during signup)
    • Specific data you want deleted (or "all data")
  4. We will verify your identity and process the request
  5. You will receive confirmation via email when deletion is complete
Send Deletion Request Email

Method 3: Social Media Data Deletion

Processing Time: Within 30 days

To delete data collected from social media interactions:

  1. Email: victor@videbimusai.com
  2. Subject: "Social Media Data Deletion Request"
  3. Specify the platform(s): Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, LinkedIn, or Medium
  4. Include your social media handle/username
  5. We will delete all interaction data associated with your account

Note: We only collect public interaction data. Private data is never accessed.

Method 4: Facebook App Data Deletion Callback

For Facebook/Instagram Users:

If you connected through our Facebook app, you can also delete your data directly through Facebook:

  1. Go to Facebook Settings → Apps and Websites
  2. Find "The Data Brief Social" app
  3. Click "Remove" or "Delete Data"
  4. Facebook will send us a data deletion request automatically
  5. We will process it within 30 days

What Happens After Deletion Request

Processing Timeline:

  • Within 24 hours: Request acknowledged (you'll receive confirmation email)
  • Within 7 days: Email address removed from mailing lists
  • Within 30 days: Complete deletion of all personal data
  • Within 60 days: Deletion from backup systems

Data Deleted Includes:

  • ✓ Email address
  • ✓ Name and contact information
  • ✓ Subscription preferences
  • ✓ Email engagement history
  • ✓ Social media interaction data
  • ✓ Analytics data
  • ✓ Any other personally identifiable information

Data Retained (Legal Requirements):

  • Aggregated, anonymized analytics (no personal identifiers)
  • Legal compliance records (if required by law)
  • Financial records (if applicable, for tax purposes)

Special Requests

Request Data Export Before Deletion

Before deleting your data, you can request a copy:

  1. Email victor@videbimusai.com
  2. Subject: "Data Export Request"
  3. We'll send you a JSON/CSV file with all your data within 14 days
  4. Then you can request deletion if desired

Partial Data Deletion

You can request deletion of specific data types while keeping others:

  • Delete engagement history but keep subscription: Specify in email
  • Delete social media data but keep newsletter: Specify platforms
  • Delete old data but keep recent: Specify time range

Verification Process

To protect your privacy, we verify all deletion requests:

  1. Request must come from the email address on file
  2. If from different email, we'll send verification to the original email
  3. You may be asked to confirm your identity
  4. This prevents unauthorized deletion requests

Third-Party Data

When you request data deletion, we also:

  • SendGrid: Remove your email from our SendGrid lists
  • Social Platforms: Delete collected interaction data
  • Analytics: Remove your data from analytics tools

Note: Data on social media platforms (Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, etc.) must be deleted directly through those platforms. We only delete data in our systems.

Consequences of Data Deletion

Please note: After data deletion:
  • You will no longer receive The Data Brief newsletter
  • You cannot undo the deletion
  • You can re-subscribe later, but previous data won't be recovered
  • You may need to re-enter preferences if you re-subscribe

Questions or Issues?

Contact our Data Protection Team:

Email: victor@videbimusai.com
Subject: Data Deletion Inquiry
Response time: Within 48 hours

For urgent requests:
Mark your email as "URGENT - Data Deletion Request" and we'll prioritize it.

Your Rights Under GDPR & CCPA

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion (this page)
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your data
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in portable format
  • Right to Object: Object to certain data processing
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Unsubscribe at any time

Complaints

If you're not satisfied with how we handle your deletion request:

  1. Contact us first at victor@videbimusai.com
  2. If unresolved, you can file a complaint with your local data protection authority
  3. EU users: Contact your country's GDPR supervisory authority
  4. California users: Contact the California Attorney General's office
