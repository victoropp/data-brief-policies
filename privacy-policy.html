Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 28, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to The Data Brief ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you interact with our newsletter service and social media automation tools.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Email Address: When you subscribe to The Data Brief newsletter
  • Name: First and last name (optional, for personalization)
  • Social Media Interactions: Public interactions with our posts on Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, Twitter, and Medium

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Email Engagement: Open rates, click rates, and email client information (via SendGrid)
  • Social Media Engagement: Likes, comments, shares, and public interactions
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our content across platforms

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Newsletter Delivery: Sending daily data-driven news analysis to your email
  • Content Distribution: Sharing insights on social media platforms
  • Engagement Analytics: Understanding which content resonates with our audience
  • Service Improvement: Optimizing content quality and delivery times
  • Communication: Responding to your inquiries and feedback

4. Social Media Platform Data

4.1 Facebook & Instagram

Our Facebook app collects the following data:

  • Your public profile information (name, profile picture) when you interact with our posts
  • Engagement metrics (likes, comments, shares) on our content
  • Page insights and analytics (aggregated, non-personal data)

We do NOT:

  • Access your private messages
  • Post on your behalf without permission
  • Collect data from your friends list
  • Track your activity outside our content
  • Sell your data to third parties

4.2 Twitter/X

We use Twitter's API to:

  • Post educational content threads
  • Monitor engagement on our tweets
  • Respond to mentions and replies

4.3 LinkedIn

We use LinkedIn's API to:

  • Share professional insights and data analysis
  • Track engagement metrics on our posts
  • Build professional community

4.4 Medium

We use Medium's API to:

  • Publish long-form articles
  • Track article views and reading stats

5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We share your data only with the following trusted service providers:

  • SendGrid: Email delivery service (only email addresses and engagement data)
  • Facebook/Instagram: Social media platform (public interactions only)
  • Twitter/X: Social media platform (public interactions only)
  • LinkedIn: Professional network (public interactions only)
  • Medium: Publishing platform (public content only)

We never sell your personal information to third parties.

6. Data Retention

  • Email Addresses: Retained until you unsubscribe
  • Engagement Data: Retained for 12 months for analytics
  • Social Media Data: Public interactions retained as long as legally required

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Correction: Update incorrect information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data (see Data Deletion section)
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from emails at any time
  • Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format

8. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure API authentication with OAuth 2.0
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls and monitoring
  • Secure credential storage

9. Children's Privacy

The Data Brief is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

10. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in the United States and other countries where our service providers operate. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers.

11. Cookies and Tracking

Our email newsletter may contain tracking pixels to measure open rates and clicks. You can disable email tracking in your email client settings. Our website uses minimal cookies for analytics.
